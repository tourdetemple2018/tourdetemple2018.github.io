Volunteer

Would you like to help make the Tour de Temple activity a success? There are many ways to contribute!

See below for all the ways you can help serve.

Attend Temple

Complete initiatory, endowment and/or sealing work for all of the deceased individuals who were baptized and confirmed by the Young Men & Young Women the morning of 11/17 at the Gilbert Temple.

Sign-up

Transportation

Help transport youth from the Gilbert Temple to the Phoenix Temple on the day of the big ride! (6 AM on 11/17)

Sign-up

Pit Crew

Drive along in support vehicles to check on the cyclists, trade out bicycles as needed, pick up and drop off other cyclists, provide food, water, medical assistance, and cheer them on to the finish line.

Sign-up

Meal Prep

Help with meal preparation (cut fruit, toast buns, etc.) and serving to our ward and cyclists as they cross the finish line at Discovery Park.

Sign-up

Bike Equipment

If you have an extra bike and/or bike helmet available to be used by other participants.

Sign-up

Cycling

Ride a portion of or the entire route with our youth and other adults.
